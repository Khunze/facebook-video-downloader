Frequently Asked Questions

Is Natorverse free to use?

Yes! Our Facebook video downloader is completely free. We support our service through advertisements.

Do I need to create an account?

No account or registration is required. Simply paste the video URL and download.

What video quality can I download?

We download videos in the best available quality, typically in MP4 format.

Can I download private videos?

Our service works best with public Facebook videos. Private videos may have restrictions based on Facebook's privacy settings.

Where are my downloaded videos saved?

Videos are saved to your browser's default download location (usually your "Downloads" folder).

Is it legal to download Facebook videos?

You should only download videos that you have permission to download, such as your own videos or public videos for personal use. Always respect copyright and intellectual property rights.

Do you store my videos?

No. Videos are temporarily processed on our servers and immediately deleted after delivery. We do not permanently store any videos.

Why isn't my video downloading?

Common issues include:

  • The video is private or restricted
  • The URL is incorrect or incomplete
  • The video has been deleted from Facebook
  • Temporary server issues

Try copying the URL again or contact us if the problem persists.

Can I download videos on mobile?

Yes! Our service works on all devices including smartphones and tablets.

How long does it take to download a video?

Download time depends on the video size and your internet connection. Most videos download within seconds.

Is my data safe?

Yes. We take privacy seriously and do not collect or store personal information. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Can I download multiple videos at once?

Currently, you can download one video at a time. Simply repeat the process for additional videos.

What browsers are supported?

Our service works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, and Opera.

Still have questions?

Contact us at support@natorverse.com and we'll be happy to help!
