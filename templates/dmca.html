DMCA Copyright Policy

Last updated: October 5, 2025

Overview

Natorverse respects the intellectual property rights of others and expects our users to do the same. We comply with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA) and will respond to valid notices of copyright infringement.

Our Role

Natorverse is a tool that allows users to download publicly available Facebook videos. We:

  • Do not host, store, or distribute copyrighted content
  • Do not upload videos to Facebook
  • Provide a technical service for downloading publicly accessible content
  • Do not control what users choose to download

User Responsibility

Users of Natorverse are solely responsible for:

  • Ensuring they have the right to download content
  • Respecting copyright and intellectual property laws
  • Obtaining necessary permissions from content owners
  • Using downloaded content in compliance with applicable laws

Filing a DMCA Notice

If you believe your copyrighted work has been downloaded through our service without authorization, you may submit a DMCA takedown notice.

Required Information

Your DMCA notice must include:

  • Your physical or electronic signature
  • Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to be infringed
  • The Facebook URL of the allegedly infringing material
  • Your contact information (name, address, phone, email)
  • A statement that you have a good faith belief that the use is not authorized
  • A statement that the information in the notice is accurate
  • A statement, under penalty of perjury, that you are authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner

Where to Send DMCA Notices

Send your DMCA notice to:

Email: natorverse.official@gmail.com

Subject Line: "DMCA Takedown Notice"

Counter-Notification

If you believe content was removed in error, you may file a counter-notification containing:

  • Your physical or electronic signature
  • Identification of the material that was removed
  • A statement under penalty of perjury that the material was removed by mistake
  • Your name, address, and phone number
  • A statement consenting to jurisdiction of federal court

Repeat Infringer Policy

We may terminate access to our service for users who repeatedly infringe copyright.

False Claims

Please note that filing a false DMCA notice may result in legal liability. Ensure your claim is valid before submitting.

Response Time

We will review and respond to valid DMCA notices within 5-7 business days.

Limitations

Please understand that:

  • We cannot remove content from Facebook - only the content owner or Facebook can do that
  • We do not store downloaded videos
  • We cannot track who downloaded specific videos
  • Our service only facilitates downloads of publicly accessible content

Contact

For DMCA-related inquiries: natorverse.official@gmail.com
