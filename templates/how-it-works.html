How It Works

Downloading Facebook videos with Natorverse is incredibly simple. Follow these easy steps:

Step 1: Find Your Video

Go to Facebook and find the video you want to download. This can be:

  • A video from your timeline
  • A video from a public page
  • A video from a group (if you have access)
  • Your own uploaded videos

Step 2: Copy the Video URL

To get the video URL:

  • Click on the video to open it
  • Right-click on the video and select "Copy link address" or "Copy video URL"
  • Alternatively, copy the URL from your browser's address bar

Step 3: Paste the URL

Come back to Natorverse and paste the copied URL into the input field on our homepage.

Step 4: Download

Click the "Download" button and your video will be processed and downloaded to your device automatically!

Tips for Best Results

  • Use the full URL: Make sure you copy the complete video link
  • Public videos work best: Private videos may require additional permissions
  • Check your downloads folder: Videos are saved to your default downloads location
  • Stable internet connection: Ensure you have a good connection for faster downloads

Supported Formats

Videos are downloaded in MP4 format, which is compatible with most devices and media players.
